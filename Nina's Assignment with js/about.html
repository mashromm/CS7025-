How to Deal with Used Clothes

Throw Away

Some people will throw away clothes as a last resort. They will only throw away clothes if they are severely damaged or have stains that cannot be removed.

However, other people will throw away clothes that have only been worn for one or two years (only if they are of good quality) because their clothes cannot keep up with the trend or their wardrobe is not large enough to accommodate too many clothes.

Selling

For many people, selling unused clothing is a cost-effective option because it reduces their own expenses and is also very environmentally friendly. However, many platforms only accept branded clothes, and it is difficult to distinguish the authenticity of the clothes on the platform.

Many people are also willing to buy second-hand clothes because they can find cheap, cost-effective clothes with good designs. But some people report that they are worried about hygiene issues, and some second-hand clothes are more expensive than ordinary clothes.
